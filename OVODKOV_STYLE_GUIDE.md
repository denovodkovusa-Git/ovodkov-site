# Ovodkov & Co. - AI Image Generation Style Guide

## Core Brand Rules (CRITICAL)
- **Materials:** 40x40mm square metallic profile tubing, expertly bent sheet metal.
- **Finish:** Matte black powder coating with a fine Moire texture.
- **Surfaces:** Pristine, polished high-grade stainless steel countertops.
- **Vibe:** Luxury Industrial, ultra-high-end, pristine, factory-fresh. NO rust, NO dirt, NO "Fallout" apocalypse style.
- **Color Palette:** Monochromatic (blacks, grays, silver). STRICTLY NO red, orange, or bright paint on the metal.
- **Lighting & Fire:** Warm yellow evening lighting (Edison bulbs, LED strips, garlands). Grills must feature LIVE FIRE (burning wood/coal), NOT gas.
- **Specs:** 1080x1080 (1:1 ratio), 8k resolution, photorealistic.
